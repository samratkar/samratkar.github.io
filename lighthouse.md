1. The degree of feeling sad is directly proportional to the circumference of the ego.
2. Life is about doing Experiments. Each bigger more complex than the previous!
3. Love deserves a chance to grow, not a reason to end.
4. Courage to see the ego killed, is the beginning of knowing what is love.
5. There is only You. There is no I.
6. Body and Mind are exquisite temples. Keep it clean by doing daily exercises, yoga, meditation, puja and eating healthy.
7. Keeping the mind clean like that white spick and span white marble courtyard of the temple! 
8. Mind can kept clean by - 
   1. doing wilful cleansing of complexities and negative thoughts daily. Make it a ritual.
   2. reading good books. 
   3. always being positive, hopeful, and a problem solver. 
   4. seeing the brighter side of things 
   5. being always in the move to connect, build, create and contribute. 
   6. being in love with someone sincerely and truly. And express your love to them in form of art.
   7. keep learning daily. art, science, philosophy, spirituality, and more.
9. Love generously. Don't expect anything in return. You will get what you need. Only that it will come from unexpected corners, when you are in the aura of love and belonging to one and all. 
10. Love is about giving. Love is about creating. Love is not about getting anything for the self.
11. The most important things to thrive in life - 
    1.  movements - exercise, gym, yoga, run, walk. daily. 
    2.  meditation daily.
    3.  sleep - have enough sleep and rest.
    4.  love - give and receive love.
    5.  learn - read books, watch videos, listen to podcasts.
    6.  create - write, paint, draw, make music, dance.
    7.  art - appreciate and perform art in any form.
