Interstellar is a 2014 science fiction drama directed by Christopher Nolan. It stars Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain, and Michael Caine. Set in a dystopian future, Earth faces blight and famine. A group of astronauts travels through a wormhole near Saturn seeking a new home for mankind. Cooper, a former NASA pilot, joins the mission with scientists Brand, Romilly, and Doyle. They explore planets affected by time dilation and face personal sacrifices. Cooper discovers a tesseract enabling communication across time with his daughter Murph, who solves the gravity equation. The film received positive reviews and won an Academy Award for Best Visual Effects.
