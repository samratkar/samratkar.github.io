1. The degree of feeling sad is directly proportional to the circumference of the ego. #ego   
2. Life is about doing Experiments. Each bigger more complex than the previous! #experiment  
3. Love deserves a chance to grow, not a reason to end. #love  
4. Courage to see the self killed, is the beginning of knowing what is love. #ego   
5. Discipline gives freedom. #penance
6. Focus on the essence. not the empty, beautiful and golden shell. focus on what is inside the shell, that is hidden. essentials are always hidden. #non-form    
7. Mind your own business. And mind your inner encounter with God, rather than judging other people.  #penance   
8. Don't reach for details at the expense of the whole.  #purpose  
9. The inner self, our heart & mind are a temple where the source of divine light resides. the thoughts we entertain, the words we speak, the actions we do, are the echoes in the alleys of this temple. it is important to keep this temple clean, fragrant and beautiful every moment, by entertaining only positive, inspiring, compassionate and loving thoughts, speech and actions. #temple  
10. The soul purpose of life is to create beauty, uncover truth and do good. And to remember that all that was just a waste of time. And that is it. Life is a wasted time anyways, as nothing matters, and all gets into the final destiny of death. But the beauty is just before death, that feeling that we toiled, worked and tried our best to create beauty, had known truths, and had done good, makes us feel at peace with ourselves. And we are ready to die. It is all about being engaged in tireless work in this path! #work    
11. Life is a lifelong preparation to die.  #die  
12. Our mind wants more. It always needs more and novel. And at the same time it needs to create. Love for learning helps as a great teddy to give to the mind. Just take as much as you want. And create as much as you want! And best part is that learning gives infinite, un-perishable, beautiful, true and useful joy. #lear
13. Learning is all that is! And nothing else actually is!  #learn  
14. All are teachers - all the time - men, women, events, institutions, time - everything and anything is nothing but a learning. #learning
15. Keep earning! It helps you to waste time in a better way, by doing good, creating beauty and uncovering truth. #earn  
16. Be free to fail. For anyways you win or lose - nothing actually matters. Same majority will hate you. And the same minority will love you - irrespective of your success or failure. So, be free to fail. For that will make you learn, and learning is all that is!  #fail  
17. Life is about 4 lines! Take the 4 lines, reflect, meditate, practice, memorize, apply, learn. And move on for the next 4 lines. For learning is all that is!  #4lines  
18. Ego is the source of all maladies. And without ego, we do not exist. The only way out then is ignore the ego. And how to? Just be lost, drugged, intoxicated. With what? Learning and Service.  #ego  
19. Just learning is not enough. Serve with what you have learnt. That makes learning more concrete, beautiful, truthful, useful and good. And that re-affirms the learning even more strongly. And you get ready to learn more. For learning is all that is!  #serve  
20. Movement makes us human. Work. Work. Work. It is through work you learn. It is through work you serve. It is through work you know. It is through work you love. It is through work that you are! Without work, you are dead. literally.  #work 
21. Love. No matter what! Even if you are abhorred and despised. As it is. All is a waste anyways. So, why not love and be wasted. Hatred is just too much of a burden. #love. 
22. It helps to be surrounded with people who hate you. They are free coaches who help you see the muck in you, what you cannot see either by your own, or through people who love you. #love  
23. All friends are needed.  All loves are needed. All shades. All forms. All hues. #love   
24. First step is to be self reliant. And then next step is to contribute. And rest of the things fall in place. #serve 
25. Reach out. For no reason. Bless, wish, greet, thank and be kind. For no reason. #love  
26. Breathe. And repeat the name of the person you love through your breath. And if you do not have someone, just pick one and start. #meditation
