And that this eternal engagement is made possible through the agency of love. It is love that makes life worth living. And all that is true, all that is beautiful, and all that is good, is made possible only through this extreme hard labor and shedding of sweat and enduring of blood of the labor of love. And yes, irrespective of the outcome, the process of engaging in this labor of love is itself the ultimate reward and fulfillment of life. For it is a beauty in making. There is no ultimate destination. As it is unbounded, endless and eternal! Where will it reach ultimately, who knows! People come and go. Events come and go. Seasons change. Heart breaks and then expands. Lights seeps in through the cracks. Darkness is replaced by light. And the light grows and grows in shades never known. In this ever changing nature of the physical life, what endures is the effort and the agency of love - thrusting that never ending stances of re-incarnation tirelessly laboring for Truth, Beauty and Goodness. And That Endures. Forever and Ever!"

### Truth Beauty & Goodness

---

![](/assets/lighthouse/truth-beauty-love-water.png)

---


When there is a toil, a sincere effort, 
Sweat shed, blood endured,
Meaning ripens quietly.
Not in the noise of victory,
But in the silent knowing
That one did not withdraw 
From the calling of becoming.

The becoming that is wrought out
With dis-interested efforts,
In service and worship.
Through the tiny gates of re-incarnations,
The self surrenders to the universal,
Expressing unlimited unbounded
Truth, Beauty and Goodness,
Through the agency of love.

No love is wasted.
Rather through hardwork and penance,
Through the sweat shed and blood endured,
Each love, as it is seeded, is whole and complete.
In its seeding! There and then!
Immortal forever, with its own story, 
with its own song, with its own symphony.

Let us water that seed. 
Let us nurture the love.
Let us hold on, and endure. 
with hard work and efforts
Letting the seed germinate, and grow
From the acorn to the huge oak,
Let us create a gallant story,
The story of our love,
The story of our life. 

The muscles gained, the strength endure,
Do come with efforts, 
But also disappears with inaction.
From this mortal realm to the realm immortal,
The turning of the tide is done
Only by the sinews of agency
To love and labor,
To suffer, sacrifice, and still
Stand and create, 
Truth, Beauty and Goodness.

In that immortal realm
Self-preservation is not the goal.
Rather the game is about preserving
The journey of evolution of the "Spirit of Life"
Through belongingness and connectedness
Preserving something unbounded and limitless - 
Truth, Beauty and Goodness. 
For the trio is so inspiring,
That it feels not only just one life,
But offer lives after lives,
At the altar of the immortal fire - 
The fire of Truth, Beauty and Goodness.

What I got, what I lost,
Seems immaterial!
For the journey is not 
that of the hand to receive,
But it is about the motion. 
adaptation, and evolution,
A journey upstream, to higher-order beingness! 
re-incarnating from lower to higher,
From mundane to profound.
Immanent – yet Transcendent! 

And all of that is fueled
But just love, and the expression thereby
Of Truth, Beauty and Goodness!
Forever and ever!
